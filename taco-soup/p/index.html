Taco Soup

via slow cooker

Ingredients

1½ lbs Ground Beef
1 Sweet Onion
1 (14.5 oz) can Diced Tomatoes With Green Chilies
1 (15 oz) can Black Beans
1 (15.25 oz) can Whole Kernel Corn
1 (10.75 oz) can Condensed Tomato Soup
3 tbsp Taco Seasoning
1 cup Water

Instructions

1
Fry hamburger and onion and drain grease.
2
Add all ingredients to a crock pot and cook for 2 hours.
